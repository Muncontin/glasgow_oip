Poster Summary

Key Highlights

Our project poster presents a comprehensive overview of the Glasgow Botanic Gardens Accessibility Enhancement Project, showcasing our innovative solutions for creating inclusive botanical experiences.

Problem Statement

Identification of accessibility barriers faced by visually impaired visitors at Glasgow Botanic Gardens

Solution Overview

Comprehensive braille integration, digital accessibility features, and enhanced signage systems

Research Methodology

Human-centered design approach with extensive user testing and stakeholder consultation

Implementation Plan

Phased rollout strategy with pilot testing, evaluation, and garden-wide deployment

Technical Specifications

Braille Standards: Grade 2 Braille following RNIB guidelines
Material Requirements: Weather-resistant polymers for outdoor durability
Digital Compliance: WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards
Audio Integration: QR code system with multi-language support

Presentation Information

Project Team

Glasgow OIP Team - Multidisciplinary accessibility design specialists

Institution

University of Glasgow | Singapore Institute of Technology | Friends of the Garden

Project Duration

3 week development cycle
