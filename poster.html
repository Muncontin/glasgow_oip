Poster Summary

Key Highlights

Our project poster presents a comprehensive overview of the Glasgow Botanic Gardens Accessibility Enhancement Project, showcasing our innovative solutions for creating inclusive botanical experiences.

Goal

Deliver an inclusive, enriching experience for every visitor to Glasgow Botanic Gardens.

Solution Overview

Braille guidebook and enhanced signage with braille text and QR codes linking to a webpage featuring more information and BSL translation.

Research Methodology

Observational research through site visits and tours to identify accessibility gaps in signage, pathways, and visitor experience.
