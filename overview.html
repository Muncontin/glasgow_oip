Solution Justification

Evidence-Based Design Decisions

Braille Integration

  • There are 20,000 braille readers in the UK
  • Supports tactile, screen-free access
  • Empowers experienced users with independence

Digital Accessibility

  • 46.9% of severely visually impaired use smartphones
  • QR codes enable scalable, self-guided tours
  • No need for on-site tech infrastructure

Universal Design Approach

  • Benefits all visitors, not just those with disabilities
  • Enhanced signage improves clarity for everyone
  • Supports an inclusive and equitable space

Analysis of Pre-Existing Solutions
