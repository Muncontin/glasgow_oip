MONSTERA

⠍⠕⠝⠎⠞⠑⠗⠁

The Monstera is a tropical climbing plant with large, heart-shaped leaves that develop natural splits and holes as they mature. Native to Central America, it uses aerial roots to cling to trees and grows best in warm, humid environments.
