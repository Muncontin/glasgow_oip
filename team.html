Our Collaborative Methodology

Our team employed a human-centered design methodology that incorporated stakeholder collaboration, iterative development cycles, and contextual research. Thus, this approach guided the ideation, testing, and refinement of solutions to ensure accessibility with long-term viability.

Solution Alignment

Our final concepts were aligned with real-world constraints including cost, maintenance, and durability. This ensured that our proposed solutions were not only user-friendly, but also feasible and sustainable long-term.
